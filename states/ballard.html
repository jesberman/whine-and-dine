Cal Ballard

Cal brings more than 25 years Senior Human Resource Management experience in complex, matrixed organizations across multiple industries. Skilled at aligning HR initiatives with business strategy, continuous improvement processes and other cost effective solutions focused on bottom-line results. He has worked for ABC Television, Citigroup, AT&T Capitol, RBS Citizens Financial and is currently an HR Consultant. Cal's consulting experience includes talent management/organizational effectiveness and executive search assignments. In addition, he has served on several non-profit boards including the Urban League of RI, National Alliance of Business and the Northeast Human Resource Association (NEHRA). Cal has a Bachelor of Arts degree from Howard University and is currently a member of the Triangle Society for Human Resources (TSHRM).
