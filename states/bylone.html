Christopher Bylone

Christopher is a Story Teller of HR Data and a workforce intelligence thought leader. As a Human Resources Practitioner with several years of experience focusing on global workforce planning and people analytics while providing excellence in sustainable leadership, he is viewed as a subject matter expert on predictive people analytics by connecting the human capital data with business intelligence, offering business leaders tangible report packages ensuring strategic decisions can be made to maximize both Top Line and Bottom Line Growth. He has been recognized as a thought leader in Human Resource Management with an appointment to the Connecticut SHRM State Council as Co- Director of Diversity & Inclusion. Christopher carries a BS in Agriculture Science from Rutgers University, as well as a MA in Educational Leadership from Eastern Michigan University and a MS in Organizational Leadership from Southern New Hampshire University.
