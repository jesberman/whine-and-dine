Jim Ullman

Jim Ullman is the founder of Spryghtly Culture and Engagement Solutions and believes that everything an organization does and everything it wants to do depends on culture and engagement. Through Spryghtly and his previous practice, Appleseed Consulting, he has consulted to a variety of non-profit, higher education, and government clients and is also a faculty member with the Center for Creative Leadership, having held several HR-related roles with FHI 360, Durham (NC) County Government, Lee Hecht Harrison, and the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Jim earned a B.A from Miami University and an M.A. from The Ohio State University. Jim holds an SPHR and is also certified in the MBTI, the FIRO-B, Workplace Big Five Profile 4.0, the Change Style Indicator™, the Change Navigator™, and as an Organization Workshop facilitator.
