Terri Kramer

Terri is a hands-on Human Resources Business Partner with a solid reputation for objectivity and integrity in meeting employee challenges in organizations. She has demonstrated effectiveness as a collaborative and resourceful HR professional with experience in technical, healthcare, consulting, non-profit and manufacturing, environments. Her specialties include coaching and counseling managers and employees, performance management, talent management, employee on-boarding, benefit administration and design, as well as compensation administration and analysis. She is a ASTD Certified Trainer and a DDI Certified Facilitator. Terri holds a BS in Business and Economics from Southern Connecticut State University.
